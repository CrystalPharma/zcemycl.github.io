Troubleshootings
  • Check the wind from the propellors. If you hold up the drone, the wind is going upwards, propellors might be installed reversed. Don't trust the mark on the propellors.
  • The motor can rotate in the wrong direction. It can be solved by, resoldering the three black wires from BLDC motor to ESC. Reverse two of three wires.
Observations
  • If you tilt the drone by lowering down the motor n below center frame, that motor will output greater power. I believe that, it is just a typical negative feedback loop.
