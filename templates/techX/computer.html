Computer Parts

Here will discuss different computer parts, and how peripherals are connected. I will use Ryzen B450 Pro motherboard as an example.

One of the most difficult tasks for setting a PC is to connect all connectors.

  1. Power Supply of Motherboard: ATX 24-Pin 12 V PSU Connector. (ATX: Advanced Technology Extended, PSU: Power Supply Unit).
  2. Power Supply of CPU: it is a 8 pins cable extended from Corsair RM750 Fan.
  3. A1425L 12S-2 Chassis (case) fan: CHA_FAN can use 3 pins cable.
  4. AMD Wraith Cooler Fan: CPU_FAN. It has 4 pins with a plate between pin 2 to 4.
  5. There are other fan marks, like SYS_FAN (system fan), etc.
  6. USB 9 pin connector
  7. USB 19 pin connector
  8. HD Audio
  9. SPK/PLED: Speaker/Power LED, 4 pin connector.
  10. PWRBTN: Power Button, 2 pin.
  11. RESET, 2 pin.
  12. HDDLED: HDD LED Indicator to show hard disk drive and SSD activity, 2 pin.
  13. Triangle sign: positive. Left: +, Right: -.
  14. CORSAIR RM 750
  15. V-NAND SSD 970 EVO
  16. Graphic card
