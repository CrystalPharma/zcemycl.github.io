Metrics

$$ \begin{align*} precision &= \frac{TP}{TP+FP} \\ recall &= \frac{TP}{TP+FN} \\ F1 &= 2 \frac{recall\times precision}{recall+precision}\\ IOU &= \frac{\text{Intersection area between 2 boxes}}{\text{Union Area between 2 boxes}} \end{align*} $$
